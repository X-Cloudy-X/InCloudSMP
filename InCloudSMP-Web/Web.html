ABOUT THE InCloudSMP S3

Main changes and tweaks:

Improved Management!

Multiple last projects have proven us the facts that there should be a separate admin account which will not interfere in the gaming process at all, introducing the first ever account to the InCloudSMP that will only be used for Maintainance.

Love for the LORE!!!

As we all know, the InCloudSMP lore was quite random, leading to unpredictable outcomes and results that had not appealed to many. Therefore, our developer team decidet to implement a new role on our discord server which will manage the writing of scripts and help with better results for the lore and the well-being of the server!

Desigh and Development

The world created and designed by TrixyBlox, as well as adopted by the developers and designers of the server, will prepare many easter eggs, more lore, and many other cool seecrets that you could possibly find, wich will definetly be one of the kind, and maybe even game breaking...

Better Maintainance!

Our new server's world spreads out across 16,000 blocks in overworld, whereas in nether and end with zero to none restrictions. The server will also be able to run smoothly even with more people joining in, due to the new hosting service.

We bare History

As the times have continuously passed on and on, we tried our hardest to keep the historical documentation of all our servers intact...
Although some notes of history have either been lost or long time forgotten making then only some urban legends that are told every now and then.
